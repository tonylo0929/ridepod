# RidePod Taxi Partner Pitch / Onboarding Script

Audience: founder, licensed taxi driver, taxi fleet operator, taxi partner tester, future operations teammate.

This script is for discovery and pilot conversations. It is not a final legal, commercial, dispatch, payment, or payout agreement.

RidePod does not provide drivers. Taxi partners are external licensed providers. Taxi Partner Quote is currently a demo/beta prototype. No real taxi dispatch, real payment, or real payout is enabled unless explicitly stated.

## 1. One-sentence pitch

RidePod helps groups form shared taxi pods first, then request one quote from a licensed taxi partner so riders can split the ride fairly.

## 2. Simple explanation for taxi partner

Instead of one passenger requesting one taxi, RidePod groups riders going in the same direction first. After the group is formed, a taxi partner can quote one price for the whole ride. If the riders accept, the ride proceeds. RidePod helps handle split payment, dispute window, and payout status.

## 3. Why taxi partners may care

- More passengers per trip.
- Higher-value scheduled rides.
- Airport / luggage rides.
- Recurring commute requests.
- Less random street waiting.
- Clearer route and passenger count before accepting.
- Potential group demand from communities, schools, and events.
- Future payout tracking.

## 4. What RidePod needs from taxi partners

- Are you licensed?
- What taxi types can you support?
- Can you support airport/luggage rides?
- Can you support larger taxis?
- Can you support electric taxi?
- Can you support accessible taxi?
- Are fixed quotes acceptable?
- How long should quote expiry be?
- How much notice do you need?
- What cancellation rules are fair?
- What payout timing is acceptable?
- What information do you need before quoting?

## 5. Taxi type checklist

- Standard taxi
- Electric taxi
- Luggage-friendly taxi
- Large taxi / van
- Comfort taxi
- Accessible taxi

Only offer taxi types the partner can actually support.

## 6. Ride request information shown to partner

Safe fields:

- Route
- Pickup area / point
- Dropoff area / point
- Date/time
- Guest count
- Luggage count
- Taxi type requested
- Accessibility request
- Safety/access mode labels
- Fare cap / estimate if available

Do not show:

- Guest phone number
- Guest email
- Gender identity
- Private profile data
- Admin notes
- Payment/card info

## 7. Partner quote flow

1. RidePod shows shared pod request.
2. Taxi partner reviews route, guest count, luggage, taxi type.
3. Taxi partner submits quote.
4. Guests accept quote.
5. Taxi partner accepts job.
6. Taxi partner coordinates pickup.
7. Taxi partner marks ride completed.
8. Dispute window opens.
9. Payout becomes ready after no issue / review clear.

## 8. Payout explanation

In the demo, no real payout is sent. In a future live pilot, payout would only be released after ride completion and dispute window/manual review.

Do not promise:

- Instant payout
- Guaranteed payout
- Automatic payout

## 9. Safety mode explanation

Women-only, Verified-only, Community-only, High-trust-only, and Invite-only modes control which riders can join the shared pod. They do not require a specific taxi driver unless the taxi partner separately supports that.

Women-only controls who can join the shared pod. It does not guarantee a female taxi driver unless supported by the taxi partner.

## 10. Questions to ask taxi partners

- Would you accept shared taxi pod requests?
- Would you prefer fixed quote or meter fare?
- What route types are attractive?
- What minimum fare is worth accepting?
- What cancellation rule is fair?
- What if one rider no-shows?
- How long can you wait at pickup?
- Can you support multiple pickup stops?
- How should luggage be declared?
- How should accessibility needs be handled?
- What payout timing is acceptable?
- What would make this not worth it?

## 11. Pilot criteria

Taxi partner pilot should start only if:

- Partner is licensed.
- Route/pricing rules are understood.
- Cancellation/no-show rules are agreed.
- Payout timing is agreed.
- Dispute handling is agreed.
- RidePod terms are ready.
- Payment/payout method is tested.
- Admin support process is ready.

## 12. What not to promise

Do not promise:

- Real payout now
- Official taxi dispatch
- Guaranteed number of rides
- Guaranteed driver earnings
- Instant payout
- No disputes
- 100% safe
- Female driver guarantee
- Legal approval already complete

## 13. Founder call script

Intro:

Hi, I am working on RidePod, a shared ride coordination product. We are exploring a Taxi Partner Quote mode with licensed taxi partners.

Problem:

Many riders want to split taxi rides, especially for airport, event, school, and recurring routes, but one person usually has to organize the group, manage the fare, and handle disputes.

Product idea:

RidePod forms the rider group first. Then a licensed taxi partner can review the shared pod request and quote one price for the whole ride. If the riders accept, the taxi partner can accept the job and complete the ride.

What we are testing:

This is beta/demo only right now. We are testing whether the quote, accept job, pickup coordination, completion, dispute window, and payout-review flow makes sense for taxi partners.

What we need from partner:

We want to understand what information you need before quoting, what taxi types you can support, what notice you need, and what cancellation/payout rules would be fair.

Ask:

Would you be open to reviewing a short demo and giving feedback? If it makes sense, we may later discuss a small pilot after legal, payment, payout, and operations review.

## 14. Follow-up message template

Thanks for speaking with us about RidePod. We are testing a shared taxi pod concept where riders group first and licensed taxi partners quote one price. This is currently beta/demo only. We would like to learn whether the quote, pickup, completion, and payout-review flow makes sense for taxi partners.
