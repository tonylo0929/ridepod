# RidePod Legal / Risk Wording Draft

## 1. Drafting disclaimer

This document is a product/legal-risk drafting aid and is not legal advice. It must be reviewed by qualified counsel before public launch or live payments.

This draft is for founder/internal review only. It should not be presented as final terms, final safety policy, final refund policy, final payout policy, or final privacy policy.

## 2. Core positioning

- RidePod does not provide drivers.
- RidePod is not a taxi operator.
- RidePod is not a ride-hailing provider.
- Taxi partners are external licensed providers.
- Depending on the ride mode, the host/organizer or taxi partner handles the external ride.
- RidePod helps coordinate shared ride pods, participant expectations, proof review, settlement status, and dispute/admin review flows.

## 3. Closed beta disclaimer

- Closed beta may use mock payment states.
- No real payout is sent unless explicitly stated.
- Proof review and admin review may be manual.
- Taxi Partner Quote may be demo-only.
- Some beta screens may show future workflow states for testing clarity.
- Beta/demo states should not be interpreted as live payment, live payout, live taxi dispatch, or final legal/commercial readiness.

## 4. Payment disclaimer

- RidePod does not guarantee refunds.
- RidePod does not guarantee reimbursement.
- RidePod does not guarantee payout.
- Charges, refunds, reimbursement, and payouts depend on the final payment policy, dispute policy, and payment provider setup.
- Off-app payments are not protected by RidePod.
- Users should not rely on RidePod protection for payments made outside RidePod.
- Payment authorization, capture, refund, and payout rules must be reviewed before live launch.

## 5. Taxi Partner Quote disclaimer

- Taxi Partner Quote is a future beta prototype unless clearly enabled for a specific pilot.
- No real taxi dispatch happens unless RidePod explicitly enables it.
- Taxi partners are external licensed providers, not RidePod drivers.
- Taxi Partner Quote does not guarantee a specific taxi partner, specific vehicle, or specific driver.
- Women-only controls who can join the shared pod. It does not guarantee a female taxi driver unless supported by the taxi partner.
- Accessibility and special vehicle requests depend on taxi partner availability and support.
- Taxi Partner Quote payout status in beta may be mock/demo only.

## 6. Safety disclaimer

- RidePod is not an emergency service.
- In an emergency, users should contact local emergency services immediately.
- Report concern is for platform review only.
- Verification, community membership, trust signals, and safety modes do not guarantee safety.
- RidePod may review reports manually and may take account action if needed.
- Safety and access modes are designed for safer matching, but they cannot remove all risk.

## 7. Proof/admin review wording

- False, altered, incomplete, or misleading proof may lead to manual review and account action if needed.
- Admin approval means approved for RidePod settlement rules, not legal proof of authenticity.
- Admin rejection means the proof does not satisfy RidePod settlement rules.
- Disputes may hold payout while RidePod reviews the issue.
- Admin notes and decisions are internal review records unless RidePod explicitly shares a user-facing summary.
- RidePod should avoid stating that fraud, a crime, or intentional wrongdoing is confirmed unless counsel-approved policy and evidence standards are in place.

## 8. Privacy wording

- Private details should not be shown publicly.
- Public profile should show limited information only.
- Reports are private and handled through manual review.
- Guest phone numbers, email addresses, gender identity, private profile data, payment details, and card details should not be exposed publicly.
- Admin-only notes should not be visible to regular users.
- Taxi partners should receive only the ride request information needed to quote and coordinate the shared ride.

## 9. Risky wording to avoid

- RidePod driver
- guaranteed refund
- guaranteed reimbursement
- guaranteed payout
- 100% safe
- 100% verified
- official taxi dispatch
- escrow
- fraud confirmed
- crime of forgery
- forever banned

## 10. Safer replacements

- Taxi partner
- External taxi provider
- Manual review
- Account action if needed
- Payout pending
- No real payout yet
- Future beta prototype
- Designed for safer matching
